Garlic Braised Chicken

Description

This garlic braised chicken recipe is a simple, flavorful dish that combines tender chicken thighs with a rich garlicy sauce.

It is a simple meal that really is greater than the sum of it's parts.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 2 pounds bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (about 4)
  • Salt
  • 20 peeled garlic cloves
  • Â¾teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1cup dry chardonnay
  • Steamed white rice, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large Dutch oven over medium-high, add enough oil to lightly coat the bottom.
  3. Season the chicken with salt on both sides, then add to the pot skin side down.
  4. Cook until the skin turns golden and crispy, 8 to 10 minutes.
  5. Flip, and sear the other side briefly, about 1 minute. Transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.
  6. Add the garlic to the schmaltzy oil over medium-high, and stir until fragrant and very lightly golden at the edges, 1 to 2 minutes.
  7. Stir in the white pepper, then immediately add the wine and 1 cup water.
  8. Scrape up any stuck-on bits from the bottom of the pot while bringing the liquid to a simmer.
  9. Nestle the chicken in the pot skin side up, cover and cook in the oven until the chicken and garlic are meltingly tender, and the wine has reduced, about 1 hour.
  10. Taste for seasoning, adding more salt if needed. Serve with rice.
